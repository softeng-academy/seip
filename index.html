YOUR LECTURE
In this course you will learn about all essential concepts of Software Engineering and its "king discipline" Software Architecture, which are relevant in practice — from the specific perspective of the software industry. The course consists of 10 lecture modules, each 3 hours long, compressed into just 5 consecutive weeks in 2024/Q4 of WS24/25. Additionally, consultation hours are offered once per week.

This course is based on the lecture concept Multimedia Didactics, which partly was developed in the context of this course and for which the lecturer received the Balzert-Preis 2023 of the Gesellschaft f√ºr Informatik (GI) "for his outstanding contribution to the teaching of Computer Science."

YOUR PARTICIPATION
The course, except for the final exam, exclusively happens online. Here, two different types of communication are used, each of which deliberately optimizes a different aspect of the course.

On the one hand, high-resolution 1080p30 video streams are used for the lectures to combine the lecturer, content, and a Head-Up-Display (HUD). The lectures are delivered as YouTube Live Events in broadcasting format and with a latency of about 2 seconds, where the display quality and features are optimized.

For technical reasons, you cannot have a direct voice interaction with the lecturer, but with the help of the application HUDS Pad, which is displayed in addition to the video stream, during the lectures you can use text messages for raising your questions, participate in the quizzes, give feedback via emojis and share your personal challenge and mood situation.

On the other hand, interactive Microsoft Teams Meetings are used, where your lecturer is delivered as a low-latency 360p30 video stream and the content is delivered separately as a high-resolution 1080p30 video stream. Here the direct personal communication is optimized, which is especially important for the consultation hours.
